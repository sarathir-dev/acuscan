Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is AI-based medical image analysis?

Our AI algorithms have been trained on extensive medical imaging datasets and validated by medical professionals. While AI provides valuable insights, it should be used as a tool to support, not replace, medical expertise.

What types of medical images can be analyzed?

Our platform supports various medical imaging modalities including CT scans, MRI, X-rays, and ultrasound images. Each type of image requires specific analysis techniques and algorithms.

How is patient data protected?

We adhere to strict data protection regulations and implement advanced security measures. All data is encrypted, and we maintain compliance with HIPAA and other relevant healthcare privacy standards.
